Rot13 ↺ Ebg13 is a tiny, no-nonsense tool for applying the classic ROT13 letter-substitution cipher right on your phone.

Type into the top field and the ROT13 version appears instantly below. Because ROT13 is its own inverse, the very same app both scrambles and unscrambles text — type into either field and the other updates live as you go. There are no buttons to press and nothing to wait for.

Features:
• Live, two-way conversion as you type
• ROT13 is its own reverse — one tool encodes and decodes
• Completely offline — your text never leaves your device
• No ads, no tracking and no permissions
• Clean, distraction-free interface
• Tiny download

Perfect for hiding spoilers and punchlines, puzzles and playful notes — or simply for learning how one of the oldest and simplest ciphers works.
